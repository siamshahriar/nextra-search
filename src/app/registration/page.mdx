# Registration and Account Activation

## Create an Account

1. **Schedule a Call**  
   On the QA Clan homepage, click the **Schedule A Call** button in the top‑right corner.

   ![Schedule a Call Form](/images/schedule-call-form.png)

2. **Submit Your Details**  
   In the pop‑up form, enter:

   - **Full Name**
   - **Company Name**
   - **Email**
   - **Phone**
   - **Additional Details** (optional)

   Then click **Submit**.

3. **Wait for Our Team to Reach Out**  
   A QA Clan representative will contact you shortly to discuss your needs and finalize your setup.

4. **Activate Your Account**  
   Once approved, you’ll receive an email containing a verification link.  
   Click that link to verify your email address, set your password, and complete your account activation.

![Enter image alt description](/images/niF_Image_2.png)

## Login

To access QA Clan, follow these steps:

1. Visit [https://app.qaclan.com/signin](https://app.qaclan.com/signin).
2. Enter your registered **email address** and **password** in the provided fields.
3. Click the **Sign In** button to log in.
