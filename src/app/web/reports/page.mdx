# Reports Section Guide

The **Reports** section in QAClan provides a detailed overview of test execution results through visual representations, such as **bar graphs**. This feature allows users to:

- **Analyze test outcomes**: Understand the results of executed tests at a glance.
- **Track trends**: Observe patterns and trends across multiple test executions.
- **Gain insights**: Evaluate the overall performance of your test suite.

---

## Exporting Reports

Reports can also be exported for further analysis or documentation purposes, ensuring that stakeholders have access to the necessary data for informed decision-making.
